About RJ Christ

With over 40 years of experience in the excavation and paving industry, RJ Christ Excavating and Paving has built a reputation for quality workmanship, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Family-owned and operated, we take pride in every project we undertake, treating your property with the same care as we would our own. Our team of skilled professionals uses state-of-the-art equipment to deliver exceptional results on time and within budget.

Whether you're a homeowner looking to install a new driveway or a contractor needing excavation services for a large project, we have the expertise to get the job done right.
